01

Full human writing, not AI paraphrasing

Most humanizers just shuffle AI sentences around. We don't paraphrase AI — we rewrite it from scratch using patterns from real human writing. The output is human writing, not AI writing with synonyms swapped.

02

Human answers, not AI answers

Other tools humanize text you already wrote. We also answer questions from scratch in a real human voice — direct, opinionated, personal. No other tool does this.

03

Better, cheaper, more reliable

We use Mistral's API — faster and cheaper than GPT-4 or Claude. You bring your own key so you pay at cost, not at markup. No subscription gouging for what should be a simple tool.

04

The iterative fix loop

Rewrite → 3 rounds of targeted fixes. Each round detects what's still AI (long sentences, hedging, dramatic phrases, Claude qualifiers) and fixes only those things. Not one pass and done.

05

Make 2–3 edits yourself

Change a couple sentences to exactly how you'd write them. No AI can replicate that. It's the single biggest improvement you can make to any output.

06

Running the server

Open terminal in the project folder and run: npm start
